# TabletPen

Turn your Android tablet into a wireless Bluetooth drawing tablet — no drivers, no cables, no host software needed.

## How It Works

1. Install TabletPen on your Android tablet
2. Tap **Pair** in the app
3. Connect from your computer's Bluetooth settings
4. Draw with your pen — it controls your computer

Your tablet becomes a Wacom-like drawing tablet. Pen pressure, tilt, and eraser are all supported. Use your fingers as a trackpad for scrolling and clicking.
